DRAGONFLIGHT


The Primalists breached the Titan prison used to hold the Incarnates for millennia. Within, Raszageth performs a hideous ritual to unleash her siblings, so together they can purge the world of the Titan’s influence. The champions of Azeroth must assault this impregnable fortress and their defenses to end this threat. While many could fall, defeat condemns all the realms to the Incarnates’ reign of fire and blood.
